Understanding Car Leases

Leasing lets you drive a new car for lower monthly payments, but you won't own it at the end.

Key Lease Terms

  • Money Factor: Interest rate expressed differently. Multiply by 2400 to get APR
  • Residual Value: Car's estimated value at lease end (% of MSRP)
  • Cap Cost: The negotiated price plus fees
  • Cap Reduction: Down payment + trade-in that lowers monthly payment
