How do I estimate home improvement costs?

Get multiple contractor quotes, research material costs, and add 10-20% for unexpected expenses. Labor typically costs 50-65% of total project cost.

Kitchen and bathroom remodels, new roofing, and energy-efficient upgrades typically provide the best return on investment.
