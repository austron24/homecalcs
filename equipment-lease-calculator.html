Equipment Leasing for Business

Equipment leasing allows businesses to use equipment without the full upfront cost of purchasing. It's popular for vehicles, technology, machinery, and medical equipment.

Lease vs. Buy: Key Considerations

  • Cash flow - Leasing preserves capital for other investments
  • Technology obsolescence - Leasing is better for fast-changing tech
  • Tax treatment - Lease payments may be fully deductible as operating expenses
  • Ownership - Buying builds equity; leasing does not
  • Maintenance - Some leases include maintenance packages

Types of Equipment Leases

  • Capital Lease - Similar to ownership, appears on balance sheet
  • Operating Lease - True rental, off-balance sheet treatment
  • $1 Buyout - You own equipment at end for $1
  • Fair Market Value - Buyout at market price at lease end
