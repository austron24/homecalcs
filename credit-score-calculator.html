Understanding Credit Scores

Your credit score is calculated based on information in your credit report. FICO scores range from 300-850.

  • 800-850: Exceptional - Best rates and terms
  • 740-799: Very Good - Above average creditworthiness
  • 670-739: Good - Qualify for most loans
  • 580-669: Fair - Subprime borrower, higher rates
  • 300-579: Poor - Difficulty getting approved

Note: This is an estimate. Your actual credit score depends on your complete credit history and specific scoring model used.
